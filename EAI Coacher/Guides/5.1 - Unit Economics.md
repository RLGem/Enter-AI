### 2.6 The Levers of Profitability

Only 4 ways to improve unit economics:

| Lever | How | Impact |
|-------|-----|--------|
| **Lower CAC** | Better targeting, organic channels, referrals | Direct: lower cost per customer |
| **Raise price** | Increase value, add tiers, annual plans | Direct: higher revenue per customer |
| **Reduce churn** | Better onboarding, more value, community | Higher LTV (customers stay longer) |
| **Reduce costs** | Optimize infrastructure, automate support | Higher margins |

**Priority order:** Reduce churn > Raise price > Lower CAC > Reduce costs
